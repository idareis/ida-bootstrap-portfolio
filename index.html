About Me

Hey there, I'm Ida and I'm based in London. I'm going into the world of front-end/web development as a junior enthusiast eager to bring creativity to the digital realm and ready and curious to learn about different concepts. I thrive on designing captivating user interfaces and seamless experiences that engage and inspire.

Beyond coding, I find solace in the enchanting world of literature. Music is my constant companion, exploring diverse melodies and rhythms that fuel my creativity. I'm also an avid visitor to art exhibitions, finding beauty and innovation in different styles. I'm discovering new freedom and excitement through travelling and discovering new cultures and languages. Let's blend technology and artistry to craft compelling digital landscapes.
